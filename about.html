About

My full name Pace Morehead Lee and I hail from Brooklyn, New York. I grew up in a neighborhood called Williamsburg which many today consider the "hipster" capital of the world. My parents are actually both artists who moved into this area in the '80s. My mother is of Korean and Japanese descent while my father is American. After graduating from high school I attended the University of Chicago and received a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science.
